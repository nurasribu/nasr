# I Have Never Regretted Listening to Myself
Blog · May 2025
topics: philosophy, mentalmodes


I have never regret any decisions I made for myself. I have always regretted trying to impress other people. Pushing my nose down closer to my chest just to fit in. Just to be one of many. I have never ever regretted my own instictive acts. I have been wrong from time to time, but I have been right more times. 

My biggest regrets are letting other people, friends or family, tell me who I should be. How I should be in my life. I will own my future. I will own my success. I will own my life.
