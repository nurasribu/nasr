# Where I am Supposed to Be
Blog · June 2025
topics: mentalmodes

For the longest time, I am trapped inside a never ending cycle of surviving. A living based on fear, hate and drive for survival's sake. Doing whatever it takes to keep your head afloat while hating yourself. That kind of life. I improved and walked a great deal of distance by being just that.

The origin of this path starts when I was on a quest of novelty. Left and right, people are trying to tell me, "Nothing new under the sun.". That is only the half truth. I know there is more than life than that because I see left and right, there is new inventions happening everyday or we could call it reinvention instead. That is better than the mundanity of life, isn't it?

All my life, making a name out of myself was never the original plan. Somehow the idea to me was to actually build something that people would recognize and deem useful, that people would know. My invention.

Back to the topic, on a random Sunday, I recognize that my life is where it should be. The next morning, the final push happened. I was laid off from my easy no shift, work from home work. My last pay was RM3800+-, and that is the only money I have now. I bought the company's laptop from my boss for RM2500, and I am left with RM1200+-. This is all I have left.

I reflected. So far I survived for years out of fear and hate. Hate for my current self. I am actually progressing, and my drive out of hate is disappearing. I could not do that anymore. I could not push through while hating who I am anymore. All I have to do now is love myself, and push through the same. Doing the same thing but coming from a different angle.

I am now doing a different approach. I want to protect my peace. I want to love what I am doing. I don't want to secretly prove a point. There's no point to prove. I am going to do it for my angry younger self while being an emotionally intelligent adult. There is nothing I would rather do than this. This is my life now. My struggle and everything.

I am exactly where I was a year ago but slightly better. In a better mindset, better equipped, better experience, no commitments, no love problems, better connections, clearer visions. This is war. A war on myself for the betterment of myself. This is something I have to win no matter what.

What I have right now is what I asked for. What I am right now is going in the direction where I want to be. Right now is the moment I live for. I live for the experience. The love, the pain, the hard work, the nights, the days, the sweats, the tears, the gains, the lost. Everything.

I am going to be who I am supposed to be.
