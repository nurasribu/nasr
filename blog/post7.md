# On Being Alone
Blog · July 2025
topics: philosophy, mentalmodes

There is something about being alone.
I have to talk to someone about it.
How they cope or they would just rather find some company always.
I think that's true for most people. There's a sense of purpose in being in a group. There's support and there's love.
People love to be around people. Being alone is yuck.
It sucks, for most people.
I like being alone. But I would rather with someone, that's true. I cannot lie I need to be around people.
